Water Filtration part of Hybrid System for Great Little Water Company

The hybrid system used by GLWC encourages efficiency and optimises heat transfer within the building's cooling system.
This is because the system reduces scaling, removes existing scale, reduces fouling and removes sludges and suspended solids.

What?

  • I was tasked to create a 3D Model of the existing water filtration section of the hybrid System.
  • I was also tasked with creating a flow simulation to show how water flows through the filtration system.

How?

  • Each part was designed and assembled in Solidworks.

Results

  • Using the Flow Simulation plug in, the water flow was demonstrated.
